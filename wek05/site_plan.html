Mora’s Music – Website Plan

Site Name

Mora’s Music

Mora’s Music is an independent music label and artist management brand. The name was selected because it is my fathers company and I will try my best in this page to see if he likes it and starts using it.

Optional domain: morasmusic.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Mora’s Music website is to provide a professional online presence for an independent music label. The site will promote artists, describe available services such as artist management and music production, and provide contact information for booking and business inquiries.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact Mora’s Music to manage or promote my music career?
  • Which artists are currently signed or represented by Mora’s Music?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Blue (#1e1e2f): Will be used for headings, navigation, and primary branding.
  • Gold (#d4af37): Will be used for accents, buttons, highlights, and borders.
  • Light Gray (#f5f5f5): Will be used as the background color.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings and titles to create a modern, bold, and professional look.
  • Roboto: Used for body text for readability and clean presentation.
